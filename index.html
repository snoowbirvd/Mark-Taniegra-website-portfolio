About me

10 years of experience supporting, leading, governing, and delivering complex facilities operations across large, global, fast-paced BPO environments.

My background spans Facilities Management, HVAC, Electrical Engineering, Operations & Process Management, Administration, Compliance, Physical Safety & Security, Housekeeping, Build-out Construction, Food Service Management, Vendor & Contract Management, Financial Control, Business Continuity, Sustainability, and Operational Leadership.

Throughout my career, I have held accountability for safety-critical infrastructure, contractor performance, financial governance, risk management, and service delivery at scale. I have led national and regional portfolios, managed multidisciplinary teams, and worked directly with executives, clients, and property owners to ensure facilities operate safely, efficiently, and without disruption.

What defines my work are the years of understanding different organizational management, processes, systems, structural pressures, and team dynamics, reflecting on what works and what does not, and aligning with frameworks through Industrial Engineering competencies.

This is my objective: bring continuous improvement in terms of structure, clarity, and alignment to our processes, information, and workforce, utilizing modern management and tools/technology to help our team deliver efficiently and be able to focus more on value-adding services.
